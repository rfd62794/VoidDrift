# Voidrift — Design Vision
**Locked:** April 2026
**Status:** Canonical — supersedes all previous vision documents
**Source:** Design conversation April 2026 — Layer 2 document. Do not overwrite with code observations.

---

## Identity

Voidrift is a space station management and fleet command game for mobile.
The player is a Fleet Commander and Station Manager, not a pilot.
The ship is a tool of command. The station is home base.
The galaxy is what you build gate by gate through permanent choices.

---

## Core Fantasy

**Survivor → Station Founder → Fleet Commander → Galaxy Builder**

The player begins adrift, discovers a derelict station, and restores it to operation.
From that foundation they build a fleet, expand through Stargates, and permanently
shape a galaxy that no two players will share.

---

## Design Pillars

### 1. Station Management (drawn from SS13 Idle)

The station has genuine department depth. Each department has inputs,
outputs, upgrade paths, and bottlenecks. Systems are interdependent.
The FORGE feeds the CRAFTER. Mining feeds both.

The station runs itself once configured. The player makes strategic
decisions, not routine ones. Automation is earned, not given.

### 2. Fleet Command (drawn from Vanguard Galaxy)

Autonomous ships are strategic assets, not just drones on fixed routes.
Base Speed + Fuel Boost model. Engine tiers are permanent upgrades.
The fleet extends the player's reach beyond what the station alone can achieve.

### 3. Exploration and Discovery

The galaxy is not pre-built. It is assembled gate by gate.
Each Stargate activation opens one new system permanently.
Discovery funds station depth — new resources unlock new departments.
No two players have the same galaxy.

### 4. Planetary Layer

Orbital stations above planetary bodies.
The player docks at the orbital station — never lands on the surface.
Each planet produces a unique resource unavailable in the solar system.
The orbital station is a specialized trading post.

Visually: large dim background circle representing the planet,
orbital station rotating slowly around it at fixed radius.

## Pacing Model

| Phase | Time | Character |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Early game | 0–30 min | Station management is mostly manual. Small galaxy. Learning the chains. |
| Mid game | 30 min – 2 hr | Automation handles routine ops. Galaxy expands through first gate. |
| Late game | 2 hr+ | Station runs itself. Player is explorer and fleet commander. |

The station grows through what the fleet discovers, not time spent at the station.

---

## Platform

Primary: Android (Moto G 2025, 720×1604 portrait)
Secondary: Android tablet (onn tablet, 1200×2000 landscape)
Touch-first. No keyboard/mouse dependency in shipping build.

---

## What This Game Is Not

- Not a real-time space combat game
- Not a game where the player pilots the ship manually
- Not a story-driven narrative game (signal strips are atmosphere, not story)
- Not a game with random or timer-reset progression

Every choice is permanent. Every station built stays built.
The galaxy you have at hour 10 reflects every decision from hour 1.
