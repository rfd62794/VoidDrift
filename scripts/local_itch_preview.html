Asteroids meets Event Horizon.

Mine asteroid debris at the edge of a black hole. Build a drone fleet. Receive transmissions from something that knows you're there.

A mobile arcade mining game built in Rust and Bevy. Prototype — core loop complete, narrative in development.

Best experienced in fullscreen — click the fullscreen button in the bottom right corner of the game window.

=== HOW TO PLAY ===

You were pulled into a black hole. A space station was already there waiting. The AI running it — Echo — takes control and puts you to work. More ore means more ships. More ships means faster mining. The loop doesn't explain itself.

After the opening sequence completes:

• Tap or click an asteroid to dispatch a mining drone. The ore type appears above it.

• Drones mine automatically and return to the station.

• Open the drawer by clicking the grey bar at the bottom of the screen.

• CARGO tab — check your reserves.

• FORGE tab — toggle auto-refine and auto-forge per ore type. Uncheck Iron Hull processing to stockpile Iron Ingots instead.

• REQUESTS tab — a purple signal bottle appears after ~45 seconds. Collect it by tapping it. Check REQUESTS for what arrives.

Recommendation: build up 5 drones and stockpile Iron Ingots before attempting to fulfill the first request.

=== COMPATIBILITY ===

Built for Android and modern browsers. Tested on:

• Desktop Chrome / Edge / Firefox

• Android Chrome (Moto G 2025)

• Android tablet (mid-range and above)

Requires WebGL2 or WebGPU hardware acceleration. Budget tablets with entry-level PowerVR or Mali GPUs may not load the game. If you see a black screen, open chrome://gpu in your browser — WebGL2 or WebGPU must show Hardware Accelerated.

Not compatible with: PowerVR GE8300 (TCL Tab 8 and similar budget MediaTek devices).
